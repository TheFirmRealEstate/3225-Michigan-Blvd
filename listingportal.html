Homesite Inspection Benefits

125 Day Warranty: Covers repairs to items found to be in good working condition at the time of inspection for 125 days.

5 Year Platinum Roof Protection Plan: Protects against new roof leaks for five years after the inspection. After deductible, repair costs are covered up to $3,000.

Mold Safe Guarantee: Covers remediation up to $2,000 for mold not present at inspection, discovered within 90 days.

SewerGard Protection: Covers sewer and water line collapses up to $4,000 for 90 days after the inspection.

Verify specific terms and eligibility directly with Homesite, Inc.

Commission Structure

Coldwell Banker Realty’s commission for the listing side is 3.6% of the final sale price plus a $495 brokerage fee.

Buyers who are represented by their own agent must specifically request any payment to their agent as part of the offer they submit. Any buyer-agent compensation, if offered, would be included within the total contract terms negotiated between buyer and seller.

This structure ensures that the seller’s obligations are clear and limited to the listing-side brokerage agreement unless otherwise agreed upon in writing.

4725 Westridge Dr – Builder / Seller Q&A Summary

Inclusions and Equipment

Q: What’s included with the property? Are the additional refrigerators and A/V equipment part of the sale?
A: All appliances can be included for the right price, including the A/V equipment (projector, screen, etc.).

HOA and Restrictions

Q: What does the HOA cover, and what restrictions apply?
A: The HOA covers maintenance of common grounds, including the entryway and areas around ponds and mailboxes. The architectural board must approve exterior changes such as landscaping or flagpoles. Additional plantings are generally fine but require approval. Flagpoles would likely be approved.

Documentation and Offer Process

Q: When will the HOA and builder documents be provided?
A: All documents (HOA, warranty, etc.) will be provided with the counter to an accepted offer. After acceptance, the buyer will have a walkthrough to create a punch list if needed.

Warranties

Q: What warranty coverage is offered?
A: There’s a 1-year bumper-to-bumper warranty and a 10-year structural warranty, which must be written into the offer.

Construction and Neighborhood Activity

Q: What are the work-hour restrictions and how disruptive is ongoing construction?
A: The City of Brookfield limits construction roughly to 7 a.m.–6 p.m., though crews are usually done by 5 p.m. and don’t work weekends. Noise hasn’t been a complaint.

Landscaping and Property Maintenance

Q: Will the damaged sod be replaced?
A: Yes, sod will be replaced in the spring if it doesn’t grow back before irrigation shutdown.

Lot and Taxes

Q: What are the setbacks and property taxes?
A: Side-yard setbacks are a minimum of 25 feet. The tax rate (mil rate) should be confirmed with the City of Brookfield.

Outdoor Features

Q: What’s planned for the opening in the back patio?
A: The square opening is designed for a fire pit, which will be installed soon.

Closing Timeline

Q: What’s the soonest possible closing date?
A: The builder can close in as little as 15 days after contingencies are met, allowing time to move out and professionally clean the home.
