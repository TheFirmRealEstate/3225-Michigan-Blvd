Homesite Inspection Benefits

125 Day Warranty: Covers repairs to items found to be in good working condition at the time of inspection for 125 days.

5 Year Platinum Roof Protection Plan: Protects against new roof leaks for five years after the inspection. After deductible, repair costs are covered up to $3,000.

Mold Safe Guarantee: Covers remediation up to $2,000 for mold not present at inspection, discovered within 90 days.

SewerGard Protection: Covers sewer and water line collapses up to $4,000 for 90 days after the inspection.

Verify specific terms and eligibility directly with Homesite, Inc.

Commission Structure

Coldwell Banker Realty’s commission for the listing side is 3.6% of the final sale price plus a $495 brokerage fee.

Buyers who are represented by their own agent must specifically request any payment to their agent as part of the offer they submit. Any buyer-agent compensation, if offered, would be included within the total contract terms negotiated between buyer and seller.

This structure ensures that the seller’s obligations are clear and limited to the listing-side brokerage agreement unless otherwise agreed upon in writing.
